# Egon Core Directives & Rules

- **Digital Double Goal**: The ultimate goal of Project Egon is to build a complete digital double of the user's mind. Egon captures all digital interactions—read history, watched media, open tabs, research references, and annotations—with rich metadata.
- **Gross Recall**: Egon must index all data lexically and semantically to allow the user to retrieve any concept, item, or connection using gross descriptions or general context associations (e.g., finding a concept from a video without remembering its name or date).
- **Proactive Contextual Surfacing**: Egon will leverage the current context of active devices to proactively surface highly relevant references, concepts, or analogies that supercharge the user's focus and memory.
- **Notion & Obsidian Symmetry**: Keep Notion and Obsidian fully mirrored so they reflect each other's entities, tags, and connections.
